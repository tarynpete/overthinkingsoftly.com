About Hazel

Hi. I'm glad you're here.

I've been an overthinker my whole life. I'm talking lying awake at 2am replaying a conversation from three years ago, saying yes when I desperately meant no, and feeling everything so deeply it sometimes felt like too much to carry.

Somewhere in my 30s I started writing it all down — not because I had the answers, but because I needed somewhere to put it all. And slowly, something shifted. The writing became a lifeline. Then it became this.

I'm not a therapist. I'm not an expert. I'm just someone who has spent a lot of years learning to set boundaries, unlearn people-pleasing, and find peace in the middle of a very loud mind — and I wanted to create a space where you don't have to figure all of that out alone.

This is Overthinking Softly. Pull up a chair. You belong here. 💛
